Car Sales Power BI Analysis

Interactive Power BI dashboard project focused on car sales performance

Power BI Business Analysis DAX Power Query Data Modeling Forecast

Project Overview

This project analyzes car sales performance using Power BI. The report is based on a full car sales transaction dataset and focuses on overall performance, sales trends, company and model performance, customer profile, dealer activity, regional performance and forecast analysis.

The original dataset contains 23,906 rows and 16 columns. Each row represents one completed vehicle sale, including information about the customer, car, dealer, region and transaction details. The report uses data cleaning, a star schema data model, relationships, DAX measures, interactive report pages, slicers, hierarchies and forecast analytics.

Business Questions

  • What is the overall sales performance?
  • How do total sales and sales count change over time?
  • Which car companies and models generate stronger sales performance?
  • How do customer groups relate to sales results?
  • Which dealer regions and individual dealers perform best?
  • What possible future sales direction can be estimated from previous data?

Dataset

The dataset combines customer information, vehicle specifications, dealer information and transaction data in one flat structure before being transformed into a clean analytical model in Power BI.

Data Model

The flat dataset was transformed into a star schema model with one fact table and four dimension tables:

  • Fact_Sales — sale transactions, prices, sale dates, car IDs, customer IDs and dealer IDs.
  • Dim_Customers — customer name, gender, annual income and income group.
  • Dim_Cars — company, model, body style, engine, transmission and color.
  • Dim_Dealers — dealer name, dealer number and dealer region.
  • Dim_Date — year, quarter, month, month name and month start.

DAX Measures

The report includes DAX measures for the main business indicators:

  • Total Sales
  • Sales Count
  • Average Price
  • Max Price
  • Min Price
  • Average Annual Income
  • Total Sales %

Report Pages

  • Sales Overview — KPI cards, sales trend, sales count and total sales by month.
  • Company Analysis — company-level sales, sales count and average price.
  • Car Characteristics Analysis — model, body style, engine, transmission and color analysis.
  • Customer Analysis — gender, income group, average annual income and customer profile.
  • Dealer & Region Analysis — dealer region, dealer name and detailed dealer performance matrix.

Forecast Analytics

The report includes a forecast on the Sales Overview page. It uses monthly total sales data to estimate a possible future direction for sales. The forecast was configured with a 3-month forecast length, automatic seasonality and a 95% confidence interval.

Tools & Technologies

  • Power BI Desktop
  • Power Query
  • DAX
  • Data modeling
  • Star schema
  • Forecast analytics
  • Interactive dashboards

What I Learned

Through this project, I practiced the full Power BI workflow: importing and cleaning data, building a structured data model, creating relationships, writing DAX measures, designing interactive report pages, using slicers and hierarchies, and adding forecast analytics to support business decision-making.
